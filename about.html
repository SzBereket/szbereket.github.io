Hi to everyone who visited my website!

After the university exam, I chose the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. I wanted my career to progress with software. I've looked at most of the software types. I focused on developing the subjects of data analysis, data science, machine learning, artificial intelligence, which I would be happy and enthusiastically do with maximum efficiency.

I am studying Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Harran University, Faculty of Engineering. I will get my Bachelor's degree in 2023. While I was studying at my university, I was entitled to the Erasmus+ program and between 2021-2022 I studied at Lublin University of Technology, Department of Electrical Engineer & Computer Science in Poland. Although I could not be very active due to the pandemic, I became interested in many software languages and created projects about them. I am currently working actively for a school project on Data science & AI.
